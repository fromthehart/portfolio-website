ACCESSIBILITY

I believe that media in all forms should be consumable, indexable, and searchable for all, inasmuch as we can achieve. When I was in my 20s, I provided audio description services for the blind as a volunteer. Throughout my 30s as time allowed, I worked on a variety of freelance audio transcription projects for a range of clients from city councils around the US to Jefferson Public Radio of Southern Oregon University, helping to make audio and video content accessible to the hearing-impaired. And now, with every web development effort, I'm committed to making accessibility an integral part of the workflow on every project.

GRAPHIC DESIGN

A long-time Photoshop tinkerer, over the years I've provided freelance editing services for professional photographers as well as designed book covers, logos, business cards, and banners for a number of published authors.

You could even call me an agent of the RGB. #designerhumor

PUBLISHING

Combining the three other disciplines, I have partnered with a number of book authors to produce high-quality books and e-books, from design and layout of the interior and exterior for both print and digital, to production of clean, accessible ePUB, MOBI and PDF formatted digital files, to assisting with navigating the technical challenges of delivering all the necessary files and metadata to Amazon, Apple and other distributors for publication.
